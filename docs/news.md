# Introducing Vibe Launcher

**Your Aurora Pulse companion — now on Android.**

We're thrilled to announce **Vibe Launcher**, the official Android companion app for Aurora Pulse. Built from the ground up with the same passion for music that drives Aurora Pulse, Vibe Launcher brings your local music experience to your phone and DAP.

## Key Features

- **Seamless Library Sync** — Wirelessly sync your Aurora Pulse library to your Android device
- **Gapless Playback** — Uninterrupted listening, just like on desktop
- **Hi-Res Audio Support** — Full support for FLAC, ALAC, DSD and high-resolution formats
- **DLNA Streaming** — Cast your music to any DLNA-compatible renderer in your home
- **Offline First** — Your music, your device, no cloud required
- **Queue Management** — Powerful queue and playlist controls at your fingertips

## A New UI Design Language

Vibe Launcher introduces a completely **new design concept** that we've been working on for months. It features a fresh, modern interface with fluid animations, adaptive layouts, and a refined visual language.

This new design direction will also make its way into **Aurora Pulse on desktop** in upcoming releases — giving you a consistent, beautiful experience across all your devices.
