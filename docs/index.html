Aurora Pulse is a local-first audiophile desktop player. Vibe Launcher is its dedicated Android companion. Together they form one ecosystem for listeners who care about quality, ownership, and conscious enjoyment of music.

Two surfaces. One philosophy.

Curate on the big screen. Listen on the go. Your library stays yours across both experiences.

Aurora Pulse

Desktop — macOS, Windows, Linux

Your mission control for music. A full-featured audiophile player built for long-lived local collections and serious listening sessions.

  • Hi-Fi playback with transparent quality info
  • Multi-band EQ with AutoEQ profile import
  • DLNA remote output to network renderers
  • DAP sync over USB/ADB with mirror layout
  • CD import with FLAC archival quality
  • Podcast discovery, subscriptions & sync
  • Library management with metadata editing
  • Smart playlists & collection curation

Vibe Launcher

Android — Phone & DAP

Your music-first home screen. A launcher that replaces your Android home with a beautiful, audio-focused experience.

  • Gapless playback — FLAC, ALAC, DSD, MP3, AAC
  • Bento-style album grid with hero artwork
  • 10-band parametric EQ & AutoEQ profiles
  • Built-in podcast client with offline support
  • DLNA/UPnP streaming to network devices
  • Full launcher with app drawer & search
  • OLED-optimized dark UI with glassmorphism
  • Zero data collection — fully offline-capable

Better Together

Aurora Pulse and Vibe Launcher aren't two random apps with a shared logo. They're one ecosystem designed for a deliberate two-screen workflow.

Library Sync

Push your curated desktop library to your phone or DAP via USB/ADB. Same structure, same quality.

DLNA Handoff

Start playback on desktop, continue on your Android device — or cast from either to any DLNA renderer.

Shared EQ Profiles

Import the same AutoEQ headphone correction profiles on both platforms for consistent sound.

Podcast Continuity

Subscribe on desktop, sync episodes to your device. Pick up where you left off on either surface.
